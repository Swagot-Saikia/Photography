About

I'm Swagot

I'm a photographer and IT student who moves between the stillness of landscapes and the relentless noise of city streets. My camera is a Canon EOS 1500D — not because it's the flashiest tool, but because constraint breeds creativity.

I shoot what doesn't wait: light before it shifts, people before they perform, nature before the wind changes. Every frame is a decision made in under a second, refined in the hours after.

Photography is how I think. Everything else is just waiting.
